Character

When you first start to play Path Of Exile There are seven different characters you can pick from. Marauder, Duelist, Ranger, Shadow, Witch and Templar, all of the have 3 different upgrade path were you can only chose one, except the Scion she as one upgrade path and you unlocked her after saving her later on it the game .Each character starts from different point in the passive tree, and starts with different skill gems and support gems. It is commonly know that the first character you build in Path of Exile is bad. Unless you follow a build guide on YouTube. After you have picked a character and named it. you will start at The Twilight Strand you start with 3 scrolls of wisdom, 2 potions that heal you, and a mana potion. Infront of you there will be a 2 socketed and linked weapon. After picking it up. You can equip it. to open the inventory press I. you will see a person on the ground with a quest, talk to him and kill the enemy for your first skill gems. After going around the area you will find a chest that is shiny and has a label on It click on it to get your first support gem, now press I and put the support gem on the weapon with your skill, you can also see if the support gems works with the skill by hovering over the support gems.

The first battle you have is against easy enemies In the location The Twilight Strand. At the end of the map there is the boss Hillock. After killing him, he drops either Normal, magical or Rare items. After picking your node. You can now go into the first town. After entering the town, you can no longer go back to The Twilight Strand, there is a Unique sword Hillock can drop where you kill him repeatedly in the first area, to farm him you make a lot of characters and go kill him after that you don’t go in to the town but logout to character selection and pick a another charterer and repeat. Defanse

There are a lot of different type of defense your character can use.

Armour: The higher the armour you have the higher your reduction to physical damage is. The higher level of area lowers your reduction to physical damage.

Evasion rating: The higher the Evasion rating you have the higher your chance to evade attacks Is. The higher level of area lowers your chance to evade.

Chance to dodge attacks: The higher the Chance to dodge attacks you have the higher your chance to dodge attacks Is.

Chance to dodge spells: The higher the Chance to dodge spells you have the higher your chance to dodge spell Is.

Chance to block attack damages: The higher the Chance to block attacks damages you have the higher your chance to block attacks damages Is.

Chance to block spells damages: The higher the Chance to block spells damages you have the higher your chance to block spell damages Is.

you can choose which defense type your character will use. But all character has the defense called energy shield. But most people thinks of it as health.

There are also exists defense called resistance against the three elements and the chaos type. The maximum resistance you can normally have 75% but this can increase with stats on items or passive nodes in the skill tree. The three elements are Fire, Cold and Lightning. The resistance reduce the damage take from the element, if you take 100 fire damage and have fire resistance at 75% you only take 25 fire damage. The elements also gives status debuffs and they can be avoided with Ignite avoidance, Chill avoidance, Freeze avoidance and Shock avoidance.
